About us

Bad Weather is an independent video game studio located in Belgium. We released our first commercial game, KAKUDO. A title that mixes the labyrinth aspect with arcade components, all supported by a mysterious scenario. Our studio is working as a support on unannounced projects, and we are currently working on our second game.
